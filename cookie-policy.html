1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

These third-party providers may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Google AdSense Cookies

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Consent
