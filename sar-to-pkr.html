💱 What is SAR to PKR?

SAR stands for Saudi Riyal, the official currency of Saudi Arabia, while PKR stands for Pakistani Rupee. The SAR to PKR rate shows how many rupees are required to buy one Saudi Riyal.

For example, if the rate is 75 PKR, it means you need 75 Pakistani Rupees to buy 1 Saudi Riyal. This rate is one of the most important financial indicators for Pakistanis, especially overseas workers in Saudi Arabia.

📊 Latest SAR to PKR Rate

The SAR to PKR rate changes daily due to currency market fluctuations. Banks, exchange companies, and online platforms may offer slightly different rates.

Generally, the rate depends on supply and demand. When demand for SAR increases in Pakistan, the value of the Riyal rises.

Always check live rates on TheCurrencyPro before making any currency exchange transaction.

Because of this, SAR to PKR rate directly impacts millions of households in Pakistan.

📈 Factors Affecting SAR to PKR Rate

1. Supply and Demand

If more people want to buy Riyals, the rate increases. If fewer people demand SAR, the rate decreases.

2. Oil Prices

Saudi Arabia's economy depends heavily on oil. Changes in oil prices directly affect SAR value.

3. Pakistan's Economy

High inflation in Pakistan weakens PKR, making SAR more expensive.

4. Remittances

Higher remittance flows increase foreign currency in Pakistan, stabilizing PKR.

📉 SAR to PKR History

Over the years, the Pakistani Rupee has weakened against the Saudi Riyal. In the early 2000s, the SAR rate was much lower compared to today.

Due to inflation, economic challenges, and global market pressures, the SAR to PKR rate has increased significantly over time.

🏦 Open Market vs Bank Rate

There are two main rates available in Pakistan:

  • Bank Rate: Used by banks for official transfers
  • Open Market Rate: Used by exchange shops and companies
Usually, the open market rate is slightly higher than the bank rate. Compare both before transacting.

📲 Best Ways to Send SAR to Pakistan

Always compare rates and fees before sending money to get the best value for your Riyals.
