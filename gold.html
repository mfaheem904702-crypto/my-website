🥇 Why Gold Matters

Gold is a key store of value in Pakistan. It is heavily influenced by the USD/PKR exchange rate — when the Rupee weakens, gold prices in PKR rise even if international prices stay flat.

📈 Gold & Currency Link

Global gold is priced in USD. A weaker PKR means higher gold prices locally. This makes gold a popular hedge against currency devaluation for Pakistani investors.
